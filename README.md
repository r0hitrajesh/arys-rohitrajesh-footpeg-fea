## Project Scope

Design and structural validation of a motorcycle rearset footpeg bracket 
under static and fatigue loading conditions.

The bracket is designed for an EV motorcycle application, fabricated from 
Aluminium 6061-T6 sheet metal. The design prioritises low weight, 
manufacturability, and structural safety under rider loading.

---

## Objectives

- CAD model a realistic rearset footpeg bracket in SolidWorks / Fusion 360
- Perform static FEA under:
  - 1.8 kN vertical load (rider weight + dynamic factor)
  - 0.5 kN lateral load (cornering force)
- Predict fatigue life at 500,000 cycles using Goodman criterion
- Propose two design improvements based on simulation results
